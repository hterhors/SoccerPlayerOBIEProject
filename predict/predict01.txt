Tonny Brochmann.
Tonny Brochmann Christiansen (born 11 August 1989) is a Danish footballer who plays as a midfielder for Sandnes Ulf in OBOS-ligaen.
He has previously played for Horsens.
Career.
Brochmann was born in Horsens and he started his career with Horsens in 2009.
